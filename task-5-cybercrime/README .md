# Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness Case Study

## Objective

The objective of this task was to research cybercrime risks and understand methods to prevent online fraud.

---

## Selected Topic

UPI Payment Fraud

---

## Description of Work

In this task, I studied how UPI payment fraud occurs and how users are targeted through fake links and unauthorized payment requests.

A case study was written explaining the steps involved in such fraud and its consequences. A prevention checklist was also prepared to help users stay safe while using online payment systems.

---

## Learning Outcome

This task helped me understand the importance of cyber awareness and preventive measures required to protect personal and financial information.
