# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Tips

- Never share OTP, passwords, or banking details with anyone.
- Always verify the authenticity of messages before clicking links.
- Use strong and unique passwords for online accounts.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.

## UPI & Financial Safety Tips

- Carefully read payment requests before approving them.
- Never approve unknown "Collect Requests" on UPI apps.
- Do not scan QR codes sent by unknown persons.
- Always confirm payment requests directly with the sender.

## Reporting Cybercrime

If you become a victim of cybercrime:

- Report immediately on the **National Cyber Crime Portal**:  
  https://cybercrime.gov.in  

- Call the **Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930** (available 24×7)

## Purpose

This checklist helps users stay alert and avoid common cyber threats, especially those related to financial fraud and digital payments.
