# Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

## Objective

The aim of this task was to create a one-page visual infographic explaining the concept of digital literacy and its importance for students.

---

## Tool Used

Canva was used to design the infographic because it provides easy-to-use templates and visual elements.

---

## Description of Work

In this task, I created an infographic highlighting the meaning of digital literacy, commonly used digital tools, and safe internet practices. The design includes icons, short explanations, and structured layout to make the content clear and visually appealing.

The infographic also focuses on responsible behaviour while using online platforms and maintaining digital security.

---

## Key Topics Covered

- Meaning of Digital Literacy  
- Useful Digital Tools for Students  
- Safe Internet Practices  
- Professional Online Presence  

---

## Learning Outcome

This task improved my ability to present information visually and helped me understand how digital literacy plays an important role in academic and personal activities.
