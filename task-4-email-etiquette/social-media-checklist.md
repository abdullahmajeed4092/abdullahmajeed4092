# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

## Do's

- Maintain respectful communication while posting online.
- Keep account passwords secure and change them regularly.
- Review privacy settings to protect personal information.
- Share useful and verified information only.
- Report suspicious messages or fake accounts immediately.

---

## Don'ts

- Do not share confidential details such as OTP or passwords.
- Avoid posting harmful or offensive comments.
- Do not accept requests from unknown users.
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links.
- Do not spread false or misleading information.

---

## Objective

This checklist helps students follow responsible digital practices and maintain a safe online presence.
